Welcome to Barkcloth Dog Beds!

Hello, my name is Jamie. Barkcloth Dog Beds is my sustainable pet project to repurpose tattered, splattered blue jeans, destined otherwise for landfill. I specialize in eye-catching, one of a kind patchwork, built from durable secondhand textiles.

I was inspired to make my first dog bed in 2016, when my adopted American Bully nibbled holes in her store-bought bed cover. Upon closer inspection, I began to notice commonly available dog beds are loosely filled (which is poor design, as exterior fabric folds make for inadvertent chewing) and composed of low quality, plastic-based synthetic textiles (also poor design, as dog paws are no match for thin cloth, and polyesters cling to hair). For fear of buying another fifty dollar pet pillow, to again encounter the same problem, I opted to patchwork a replacement cover (this time with a side zipper, for easy removal when wash or repair was needed) from a pile of outgrown Wrangler and Roper jeans my mother had saved over a decade on our family cattle farm.

Succeeding with that repurposed denim (which has aged handsomely and continues to be exponentially stronger & longer lasting in second life over the store-bought original) has been an ongoing awakening for me:

  • Have a vision for better quality and better design? Make it yourself!
  • The belongings we discard aren’t trash. They’re a resource!
  • Overwhelmed by the urgent, global issues involving excess waste & climate change? Examine your own life, start small, and begin adapting your everyday objects. Baby steps, over time, can make significant impact!
  • Love your pet and love your life? You can own a dog bed as unique as both of you are!

I look forward to sharing my passion with you and your dog as well. Thank you for stopping by!
